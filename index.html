Rules of the game | Controls

Just pave the way with non-negative result (i.e. zero or greater). Each move shifts down your Rhomb and increases or decreases your current sequence by the specified value in tile. When your Rhomb reaches lower tile next move will lift Rhomb up and continue the game if your current sequence is not negative, otherwise the game will is over. Score of the game updates only when Rhomb lifts up. Good luck!

Rules of the game | Controls

  • Move - use left and right arrows, A or D keys or left-right swipe.
  • Restart - tap Try again or press R or N key.
